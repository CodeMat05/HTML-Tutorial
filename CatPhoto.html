Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish known for its rich, savory, and slightly tangy flavor. Made by simmering chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns, this recipe is loved for its simplicity and delicious taste. Chicken Adobo is easy to prepare, requires few ingredients, and is perfect for everyday meals, making it one of the most popular comfort foods in the Philippines.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Whole peppercorns
  • Water
  • Cooking oil
  • Sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the chicken by washing and cutting it into serving pieces.
  2. In a bowl, combine the chicken, soy sauce, and garlic, then marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat cooking oil in a pan and lightly brown the chicken.
  4. Add the marinade, vinegar, bay leaves, peppercorns, and water.
  5. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and let it simmer until the chicken is tender.
  6. Add a small amount of sugar if desired and adjust the seasoning to taste.
  7. Continue simmering until the sauce slightly thickens.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice.
